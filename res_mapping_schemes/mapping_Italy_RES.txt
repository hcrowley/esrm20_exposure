The exposure model for Italy was provided by Barbara Borzi (barbara.borzi@eucentre.it) and Marta Faravelli (marta.faravelli@eucentre.it) based on the 2001 ISTAT census data. The variables in the census have been mapped to the GEM taxonomy as follows:


Mat		Piani		GEM Taxonomy
muratura	1-3		MUR+STRUB/LWAL+CDN/H1 to H3
muratura 	4-8		MUR+STDRE/LWAL+CDN/H1 to H8
ca		1-8		CR/LFINF+.../H1 to H8
pilot		1-8		CR/LFINF+.../SOS/H1 to H8
altro		1-8		MCF/LWAL+CDL/H1 to H8


Epoca		GEM Taxonomy (for CR/LFINF)
pre 1919	CDN
1919-1945	CDL
1946-1961	CDL
1962-1971	CDL
1972-1981	CDL
1982-1991	CDL
dopo 1991	CDL

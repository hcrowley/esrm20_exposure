The exposure model for Monaco is taken from GED4GEM (see https://platform.openquake.org/exposure/) and uses the NERA mapping scheme (that has been made available in this repository, i.e. https://gitlab.seismo.ethz.ch/efehr/esrm20_exposure/tree/v0.2).
